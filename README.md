### Presentation Details

* **Title:** *Anomaly Detection in Neural Networks via One-Class Support Vector Methods*
* **Conference:** Joint Statistical Meetings (JSM) 2025
* **Presentation Type:** Oral 
* **Author:** Poorna Sandamini Senaratne & Edgard M. Maboudou-Tchao
* **Affiliation:** Department of Statistics and Data Science, University of Central Florida

### Abstract

The presentation introduces a framework for anomaly detection using one-class support vector methods (SVDD / LS-SVDD) applied to neural network embedding layers. The approach is designed to detect distributional shifts and outliers in high-dimensional settings and is evaluated through simulation studies and real-world datasets.
